Best GeoGuessr Alternative

#1 – GeoBrawl

If you're looking for the best alternative to GeoGuessr, GeoBrawl stands out as a strong competitive option.

Unlike traditional random-location guessing games, GeoBrawl introduces a strategic multiplayer format. One player becomes the Picker and intentionally selects a Street View location for others to guess.

This creates psychological gameplay, deception, and tactical decision-making — transforming geography knowledge into a competitive challenge.

✔ Player-picked Street View locations
✔ Rotating roles each round
✔ Browser-based gameplay
✔ Free to play

How It Compares to GeoGuessr

GeoGuessr popularized the Street View guessing genre. Players are dropped into random real-world locations and must pinpoint where they are on a map.

GeoGuessr focuses primarily on solo exploration and deduction, while GeoBrawl adds strategy through player-selected locations.

Other GeoGuessr Alternatives

City Guesser
Uses real-world video footage instead of Street View. Players guess the city or country based on visual clues from walking or driving videos.

Seterra
A traditional geography quiz platform focused on labeling countries, capitals, and regions on static maps. Ideal for educational practice.
